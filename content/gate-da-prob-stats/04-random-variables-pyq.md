## Q5009

[MCQ]
[PYQ: GATE DA 2025]

Suppose $X$ and $Y$ are random variables. The conditional expectation of $X$ given $Y$ is denoted by $E[X \mid Y]$. Then $E[E[X \mid Y]]$ equals

- **(A)** $E[X \mid Y]$
- **(B)** $\dfrac{E[X]}{E[Y]}$
- **(C)** $E[X]$
- **(D)** $E[Y]$

**Answer:** C
