## Q5016

[MCQ]
[PYQ: GATE DA 2025]

A continuous random variable $X$ has CDF:
$$
F_X(x) = \begin{cases} 0 & x < 0 \\ x^2 & 0 \le x \le 1 \\ 1 & x > 1 \end{cases}
$$
The median of $X$ is:
- **(A)** $1/\sqrt{2}$
- **(B)** $1/2$
- **(C)** $1/4$
- **(D)** $\sqrt{2}$

**Answer:** A
